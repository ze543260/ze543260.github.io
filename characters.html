Characters

  • Buck: A large, strong dog who is the protagonist of the story
  • John Thornton: A kind and generous man who becomes Buck's master
  • Hal: A cruel and abusive man who is Thornton's rival for Buck's ownership
  • Charles: Hal's older brother, who is also cruel to Buck
  • Mercedes: Hal and Charles's sister, who is also cruel to Buck
  • Spitz: A vicious sled dog who is Buck's main rival
